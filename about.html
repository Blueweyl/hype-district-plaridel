Our Story

Experience to Believe the Hype

Hype District Barbers started as a barbershop in La Loma, Quezon City (141 N.S. Amoranto cor. Isarog St.), built on a simple idea: street-style energy meets classic barbering craft. That philosophy grew the flagship into a name trusted by artists, rappers, and local personalities — with 51,000+ Facebook followers and a TikTok following of 391K+ racking up 7M+ likes.

The Plaridel branch, in Barangay Tabang, Bulacan, is our second location — bringing that same experience to a new community, with its own growing following of 4,600+ on Facebook and a reputation for always being open when you need a cut.
