Our Story

From La Loma, Quezon City to Plaridel, Bulacan

Hype District Barbers began as a premium, street-style-meets-classic-barbering destination at 141 N.S. Amoranto cor. Isarog St., La Loma, Quezon City — built around the tagline "Experience to Believe the Hype." The flagship has since grown into a name trusted by artists, rappers, and local personalities, with 51,000+ Facebook followers and 391K+ TikTok followers racking up 7M+ likes.

The Plaridel branch is Hype District's second location — bringing that same grooming experience to Bulacan with its own growing following of 4,600+ on Facebook.
